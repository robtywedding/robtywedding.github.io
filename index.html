How We Met

First comes Facebook, then comes marriage

Our journey is a testament to the power of true love and how it can endure the test of time and distance. Robin and Tyler first connected on Facebook on August 8th, 2010. They dated for eight months before the distance between their cities and the trials of life caused them to part ways. However, despite this separation, they both carried a torch for each other and hoped to someday find a way back to each other's arms. After seven long years of being apart, fate intervened, and they serendipitously met again in June 2017. Their first conversation lasted for seven hours, during which they caught up on everything that had happened in their lives. Tyler had become a father, and Robin had just left a relationship. Tyler welcomed Robin back into his life with open arms, and she was thrilled to reconnect. Although they initially started off as friends, their undeniable connection and everlasting love brought them back together. Robin quickly fell in love with Tyler’s son Nathan and his entire family. Their love story has been full of many adventures, trips, and shared experiences, both good and bad. They supported each other through tough times and celebrated many happy moments together. They share a love for family, friends, food, and travel. Finally, on August 8th, 2021, exactly 11 years after they first met, Robin and Tyler welcomed their beautiful daughter, Sierra, into the world. It was a magical moment, and they both knew that their love had come full circle. Our love story is nothing short of beautiful, and we are thrilled to celebrate with all of you! True love is worth fighting for, no matter the distance or time apart. With love, Robin & Tyler
